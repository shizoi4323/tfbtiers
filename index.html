Ranking System

The overall ranking is based on the total score accumulated across the different categories on the platform, placing you in a ranking for each one. It updates instantly and continuously. There are 8 different categories, each with a maximum of 60 points.

Tier Breakdown

Each tier ranges from Low Tier 5 to High Tier 1. This varies depending on the category: for 1v1 and 4v4, a tester evaluates players by sending a request and testing each one, and based on that, a tier is assigned. Stats are scored based on matches played separately between players of the same tiers. Control is evaluated by a specialized team responsible for player assessment. Finally, leagues are ranked according to your performance in them.

Regions & Modes

In 1v1, 4v4, and stats matches, players will be matched within the same region to ensure fair and more competitive games. Additionally, there is a classification based on the number of points you have: Rookie, Cadet, etc.
